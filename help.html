Help / FAQ

What is Watchlist & Alerts (lite)?

A simple way to follow specific tokens. When you Watch a token, Metridex checks it periodically and sends lightweight alerts on notable changes (price Δ, liquidity shifts, holder concentration, LP-lock milestones, major flags). It’s designed to be useful yet quiet.

How do I enable it?

In the bot, open a token report and tap Watch. You’ll get a confirmation. To stop, tap Unwatch in the same place.

What alerts will I receive?

  • Price Δ thresholds (e.g., ±5%, ±10%, 24h moves).
  • Liquidity adds/removes over a threshold.
  • Holders concentration shifts.
  • LP lock upcoming/expired milestones.
  • Major flags (e.g., new proxy/admin risks detected).

How often are checks made?

We use a conservative cadence (and cooldowns) to avoid spam. Expect a few alerts per day per watched token at most, only on material changes.

How do I mute alerts?

Tap Unwatch in the token card, or use /watchlist to manage subscriptions.

Limits & availability

Watchlist is lite in MVP: coverage for ETH/BSC/Polygon, reasonable caps per user, and guardrails to prevent spam. Pro tiers increase limits.
