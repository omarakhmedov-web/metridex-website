About

Metridex is more than a landing page or a scoring script. The repos include a Telegram-first product with callback flow, tariff logic, report export, domain checks, on-chain inspection, watchlist logic, and a deployable webhook stack. For an acquirer, that means less rebuilding and faster time-to-market.
